# Role Definitions

This document defines the key roles typically present within an enterprise operating model.

Clear role definitions reduce confusion and improve accountability across teams.

---

## Executive Leadership

Responsible for defining strategic direction and approving major investments.

Key responsibilities:

- defining strategic objectives
- approving major initiatives
- resolving high-level conflicts

---

## Governance Leaders

Governance leaders ensure initiatives align with strategy and organizational priorities.

Key responsibilities:

- evaluating initiatives
- overseeing portfolio health
- managing cross-program coordination

---

## Program Leaders

Program leaders coordinate large initiatives that require collaboration across teams.

Key responsibilities:

- aligning delivery teams
- managing dependencies
- tracking risks and milestones

---

## Delivery Teams

Delivery teams implement the technical or operational work required to deliver initiatives.

Key responsibilities:

- executing project tasks
- delivering milestones
- providing progress updates
