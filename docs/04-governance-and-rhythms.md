# Governance and Communication Rhythms

This document explains the meeting structures and communication rhythms used to coordinate large organizations.

Regular communication rhythms help maintain alignment across leadership, programs, and delivery teams.

---

## Typical Governance Cadence

Organizations often establish the following cadence:

Weekly  
Program coordination meetings

Biweekly  
Cross-team dependency reviews

Monthly  
Executive steering committee

Quarterly  
Strategic review and initiative prioritization

---

## Purpose of Governance Rhythms

Governance rhythms allow organizations to:

- monitor progress
- resolve issues
- adjust priorities
- maintain leadership visibility

Consistent rhythms reduce communication gaps and improve execution discipline.
